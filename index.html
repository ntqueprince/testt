ENGLISH SCRIPT

Main Script:

Sharing a quick offer that could help you save up to 40% on your next car service — may I take 30 seconds to explain?

We’re currently running a special service program with our partner garage network where you get a free car health check-up, and if you go ahead with servicing, we complete it within just 60 minutes (excluding washing).

The best part — our customers are saving up to 40% compared to branded workshops with genuine parts, trained technicians, and complete warranty on service quality.

(Add Value Hook) You also get updates at every step, and we even offer free pickup & drop for select services — so you don’t have to spend your day waiting at the garage.

(Closing CTA) Would you like me to schedule a call back with our garage servicing team?

Also, please help me with your last car service month and year, and if you remember your odometer reading, please share that too.

If customer says “No”:

Savings Hook (Money Angle) — Understood, Sir/Ma’am. Just letting you know — most of our customers switched because they were spending 30–40% more at their old workshop for the same quality.

The free car health check-up alone helps spot issues early and costs nothing — would you like me to just book that for you?

If customer says “No” again:

Totally fine, Sir/Ma’am. If you ever plan a service in the future, do keep our garage network in mind — we offer up to 40% savings and 1-hour quick service with a free car health check-up.

You can reach us anytime on our toll-free number. Thank you for your time!
